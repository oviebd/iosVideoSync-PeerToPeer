# Privacy Policy for Local Video Sync

**Last Updated:** March 14, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to **Local Video Sync** ("we," "our," or "the app"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Local Video Sync application.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. By using Local Video Sync, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Local Content:** Local Video Sync is designed to work primarily with content stored on your device. We do not upload, transmit, or store your videos or media files on external servers.

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information:** We may collect basic device information (such as device type, operating system version) to ensure compatibility and improve app performance.
- **Usage Data:** We may collect anonymous usage statistics to understand how the app is used and to fix issues.

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect personal identification information (such as name, email, or phone number) unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., for support).
- We do not collect, store, or transmit your video content to external servers.
- We do not track your location for advertising purposes.

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide the Service:** Enable local video syncing and peer-to-peer functionality between your devices.
2. **Improve the App:** Fix bugs, optimize performance, and develop new features.
3. **Support:** Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support when requested.
4. **Legal Compliance:** Comply with applicable laws and regulations.

---

## Local Network & Peer-to-Peer Connectivity

Local Video Sync uses local network connectivity to sync videos between devices. This may involve:

- **Local Network Access:** The app may request access to your local network (Wi-Fi) to discover and connect to other devices for syncing.
- **Bluetooth (if applicable):** Some features may use Bluetooth for device discovery.
- **Data Stay Local:** Synced content remains on your local network and is not transmitted over the internet to our servers.

---

## Data Storage & Security

- **Local Storage:** Your videos and sync data are stored locally on your device(s).
- **No Cloud Storage:** We do not store your content on our servers.
- **Security:** We implement appropriate technical measures to protect data processed by the app. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

---

## Third-Party Services

Local Video Sync may integrate with third-party services (e.g., analytics, crash reporting). These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their policies. We only use services that are committed to protecting user privacy.

---

## Children's Privacy

Local Video Sync is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- **Access:** Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
- **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate data.
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your data.
